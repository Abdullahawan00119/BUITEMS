Faq's

General frequently asked questions

Our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section is designed as a quick and convenient resource for all visitors to the BUITEMS website. We understand that navigating university processes and information can sometimes be complex, so we've compiled answers to the most common inquiries regarding admissions, academic programs, student life, financial aid, and administrative procedures.

Our aim is to provide immediate, clear, and comprehensive responses, empowering you to find the information you need efficiently. We regularly update this section to ensure accuracy and relevance, reflecting the latest policies and services available at BUITEMS.

Have Any Question ?

The admission process typically involves selecting your desired program, submitting an online application along with required documents, and appearing for an entrance test (NTS/HAT valid results are often accepted). Admission is merit-based, calculated from your academic record and test score.

BUITEMS offers various financial assistance options, including merit scholarships (after the first semester), work-and-study programs, fee concessions for needy students, fee concessions for siblings, and fee installments. Scholarships like USAID need-based scholarships may also be available.

Fee payments can often be made through computerized bank fee challans, online banking apps (e.g., HBL Mobile Banking App), or demand drafts. Ensure all required documents are submitted and verified at the admission office for valid admission.

To cancel a submitted request (e.g., application, document request, service request), please refer to the specific instructions provided within the original request process. If no direct cancellation option is available, you should immediately contact the relevant department or administrative office (e.g., Admissions, Registrar's Office) through their official contact channels, providing your full details and the request reference number. They will guide you on the necessary steps.
